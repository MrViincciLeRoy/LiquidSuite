About Categories

Categories help map your bank transactions to ERPNext accounts. Each category includes keywords that automatically match transactions.

Tips for Keywords:
  • Use comma-separated keywords (e.g., "uber, bolt, taxi")
  • Keywords are case-insensitive
  • Use common terms that appear in transaction descriptions
  • Be specific to avoid false matches
Transaction Types:
  • Expense: Money going out (debits)
  • Income: Money coming in (credits)
  • Transfer: Internal transfers between accounts
